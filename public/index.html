Big Mac Big Map

An up-to-the-hour look at Big Mac prices around the world.

The Big Mac Index, created by The Economist, uses a Big Mac to measure the Purchasing Power Parity of a country. Essentially, this means $1 of goods should get you an equal amount of goods in any country. However, this is not the case so it shows how undervalued or overvalued a currency is.

Pick a Year



Change the colors of the map to indicate changes in prices from year to year.

Current Prices

Select "now" as the year and hover over any colored country for up to the hour exchange rate information and Big Mac dollar values for each country.

Historical Prices

Click any colored country for more information about historical Big Mac Prices from the last 15 years. Drag to compare multiple countries side by side.

Price Differences

Graphical representation of the price of a Big Mac in the US in the selected year minus the cost of the Big Mac converted to USD in another country.
